Robin Hood Army is a volunteer-based organization that redistributes surplus food from restaurants, weddings, and functions to the less fortunate.

Robin Hood Army is a zero-funds organization dedicated to addressing hunger and food wastage by redistributing surplus food to the needy. Operating across multiple cities in India, its volunteers, known as "Robins," collect excess food from restaurants, weddings, and events.

They ensure that this food reaches homeless and hungry individuals, transforming what would have been waste into valuable resources for the underprivileged.

Beyond food distribution, Robin Hood Army also engages in educational drives and healthcare initiatives, aiming to create self-sustaining communities. Their mission is to build a community-driven movement that effectively addresses hunger and promotes social welfare.
