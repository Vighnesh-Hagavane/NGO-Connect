No Food Waste is an NGO focuses on recovering surplus food from weddings, parties, and other events to feed the hungry.

No Food Waste is an NGO dedicated to reducing food wastage by recovering and redistributing surplus food from weddings, parties, and other events. The organization operates in multiple cities, collecting excess food that would otherwise go to waste and delivering it to those in need.

Their efforts help bridge the gap between food surplus and hunger, ensuring that nutritious food reaches underprivileged individuals and families.

No Food Waste also works on raising awareness about food wastage and promoting sustainable practices within communities. By collaborating with volunteers, event organizers, and local authorities, they strive to create a more efficient and compassionate food system.
