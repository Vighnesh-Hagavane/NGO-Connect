Annakshetra, based in Jaipur, is an NGO committed to combating food wastage by redistributing leftover food from events such as weddings and parties to the needy.

The organization works closely with event organizers and volunteers to collect surplus food and ensure its timely distribution to underprivileged communities. Annakshetra's initiatives not only provide immediate relief to the hungry but also promote awareness about the importance of food conservation.

By fostering collaborations with local businesses and community members, Annakshetra aims to build a sustainable network that addresses hunger and reduces food waste, ultimately contributing to a more equitable society.
