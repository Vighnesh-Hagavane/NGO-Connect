People for Animals (PFA) is one of India's largest and most influential animal welfare organizations, dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation, and care of sick and needy animals. Founded by Maneka Gandhi, PFA operates a network of shelters, ambulances, and disaster rescue missions across the country.

Their initiatives include veterinary care, animal adoption, and awareness programs to educate the public about animal rights and welfare.

PFA also advocates for stronger animal protection laws and collaborates with government agencies and other NGOs to address issues such as illegal wildlife trade and cruelty to animals. By mobilizing volunteers and resources, PFA strives to create a compassionate and humane environment for all animals in India.
